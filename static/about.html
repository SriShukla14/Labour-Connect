Special Thanks

Labour Connect is the result of dedicated efforts from a passionate team and the generous support of many valued contributors. We would like to express our heartfelt thanks to the open source community, local laborers, early users, and partner organizations who helped shape this platform. Their feedback, expertise, and encouragement have been invaluable in creating a service that truly serves the needs of both workers and those seeking labor. Without their collaboration, this project would not have been possible.

We are especially grateful to those who have contributed code, reported issues, designed UX improvements, and advocated for fair labor practices. Their spirit of openness and collaboration embodies the core values of Labour Connect. We also thank our user community for their trust and active participation in building a trustworthy and accessible labor marketplace.
